Our Story
Four generations of garment care.

Art Baryames, a Greek immigrant from a village near Sparta, founded Baryames Cleaners in 1922 as a humble shoe shine and hat-cleaning stand on Washington Avenue in Downtown Lansing.

Over a century later, the Baryames family still leads the business. What started as a one-man stand has grown into Mid-Michigan's largest and most trusted dry cleaning operation, with 11+ locations and a fleet of pickup and delivery vehicles serving the entire Greater Lansing area.

Three generations of Baryames have run this company with the same principle: treat every garment like it's your own.
